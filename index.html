About Me!


Hello! I'm Thomas Mastantuono. I'm a graduate from the University of Waterloo with a BASc degree in Management Engineering.

I've had an interest in programming my whole life and taught myself to code when I was 12. I've been making small games and applications ever since.

More recently I have been expanding my skillsets to do more than just code. I've learned a lot about Statistics, Math, Game Design, Project Management, and Business as I explore my two main projects Pragmatica and Frigid Blackwaters.

I'm currently open to work in the tech field and have interest in a wide array of jobs. I'm looking for positions in software development, project management, and game design. If you like my work, feel free to reach out!
